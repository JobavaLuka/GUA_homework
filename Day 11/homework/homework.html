Lion

Meet the king of the jungle! Lions are powerful big cats known for their golden manes and mighty roar.
They live in groups called prides and spend much of their day resting in the sun.

Tiger

This striped predator is the largest of all big cats. Tigers are stealthy hunters with powerful muscles and sharp eyesight.
Though solitary by nature, they rule their territory with quiet strength and grace. More information

Rhinoceros

With thick skin and a horned nose, rhinos look like prehistoric giants.
These gentle herbivores spend their days grazing and wallowing in mud to keep cool and protect their skin.

More information

Gorilla

Gorillas are intelligent, social primates that live in tight-knit family groups.
Despite their size, they are peaceful and gentle, communicating through gestures, sounds, and even facial expressions.

More information

Elephant

The largest land animal on Earth! Elephants are highly intelligent and emotional,
using their trunks to grab food, greet friends, or even give themselves a shower.

Giraffe

The tallest animal on the planet, giraffes use their long necks to reach leaves high up in trees.
Their gentle nature and graceful walk make them a favorite among zoo visitors.

Zebra

Zebras may look like wild horses, but their bold black-and-white stripes help them blend into the herd.
