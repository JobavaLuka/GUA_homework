Deftones

Deftones' albums:

    Ohms

    Ohms marks a confident return, blending shoegaze-inspired textures with crushing riffs and sharp dynamics. The lyrics wrestle with themes of disconnection, decay, and perseverance, while the music balances
    melody and heaviness. It feels both reflective and urgent, reaffirming Deftones' ability to evolve without losing the intensity of their core identity.

    Songs on the album:

    1. Genesis
    2. Ceremony
    3. Urantia
    4. Error
    5. The Spell of Mathematics
    6. Pompeji
    7. This Link Is Dead
    8. Radiant City
    9. Headless
    10. Ohms
    Listen on Spotify

    Gore

    Deftones pushed their sound into dreamlike territory with Gore, layering shimmering, reverb-soaked guitars over complex rhythms. It balances aggression and atmosphere, with moments of soaring beauty and
    abrasive heaviness. The album highlights the band's willingness to experiment, creating a lush yet unsettling sonic landscape that feels both fragile and powerful.

    Songs on the album:

    1. Prayers/Triangles
    2. Acid Hologram
    3. Doomed User
    4. Geometric Headdress
    5. Hearts/Wires
    6. Pittura Infamate
    7. Xenon
    8. L(MIRL)
    9. Gore
    10. Phantom Bride
    11. Rubicon
    Listen on Spotify

    Koi No Yokan

    Koi No Yokan expands Deftones' signature mix of heaviness and atmosphere with a strong romantic undercurrent. The album flows between crushing riffs, lush textures,
    and dreamlike melodies, exploring themes of inevitability, love, and emotional intensity. It feels both aggressive and intimate, making it one of the band's most immersive and balanced records.

    Songs on the album:

    1. Swerve City
    2. Romantic Dreams
    3. Leathers
    4. Poltergeist
    5. Entombed
    6. Graphic Nature
    7. Tempest
    8. Gauze
    9. Rosemary
    10. Goon Squad
    11. What Happend to You?
    Listen on Spotify

    Diamond Eyes

    Created after personal tragedy, Diamond Eyes shines with hope, resilience, and crushing beauty. The album delivers huge, groove-heavy riffs alongside soaring, ethereal choruses.
    Its polished yet raw sound captures the band's rebirth, offering both catharsis and energy, and stands as one of their most uplifting yet heavy releases.

    Songs on the album:
